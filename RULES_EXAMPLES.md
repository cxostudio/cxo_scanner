# Example Rules (Image se liye gaye)

Aap image me jo questions dikh rahe hain, unhein is tarah se rules me add kar sakte hain:

## Section 1: Value Proposition and Key Messaging

**Rule 1:**
- **Title:** Hero Section Benefits
- **Description:** Does the hero section highlight the main benefits of shopping with you (e.g., "Easy returns", "Free shipping", "Satisfaction guaranteed")?

**Rule 2:**
- **Title:** Hero Section 5-Second Test
- **Description:** Has the hero section been tested using a 5-second test to ensure key messages are clear?

**Rule 3:**
- **Title:** Value Proposition Statement
- **Description:** Is the value proposition clearly stated on the homepage (e.g., with a tagline or welcome blurb)?

## Section 2: CTAs and Key Navigation

**Rule 4:**
- **Title:** Hero Section CTAs
- **Description:** Does the hero section contain one or two visually prominent CTAs above the fold with relevant copy (e.g., "Shop Now", "Explore Collections")?

**Rule 5:**
- **Title:** Special Offers Highlighted
- **Description:** Are specific deals, special offers, or urgency offers highlighted near the top of the homepage?

**Rule 6:**
- **Title:** Product Categories Display
- **Description:** Are the most important product categories shown first, with descriptive photos near the top of the homepage?

**Rule 7:**
- **Title:** Special Category Pages
- **Description:** Does the store use special category pages (e.g., best-sellers, new arrivals, sales) that take users into a shopping mode?

## Section 3: Page Layout and Design

**Rule 8:**
- **Title:** No Carousel in Hero
- **Description:** Does the homepage hero section avoid using a carousel (slider)?

**Rule 9:**
- **Title:** Hero Section Visual Appeal
- **Description:** Is the hero section visually appealing and optimized for loading speed?

**Rule 10:**
- **Title:** High-Quality Graphics
- **Description:** Does the homepage contain meaningful high-quality graphics, not clip art or generic images?

**Rule 11:**
- **Title:** Professional Design
- **Description:** Is the homepage professionally designed, not overloaded, and does it create a positive first impression?

## Section 4: Promotions and Offers

**Rule 12:**
- **Title:** Site-Wide Offers Promotion
- **Description:** Does the homepage promote site-wide offers at the top of the page (e.g., Free Shipping) with urgency and scarcity triggers?

## Section 5: Product and Social Proof

**Rule 13:**
- **Title:** PR Exposure Logos
- **Description:** Does the homepage highlight logos of news sites/blogs/celebrities where the brand has had any PR exposure?

**Rule 14:**
- **Title:** User-Generated Content
- **Description:** Does the homepage display user-generated photos or videos (from social media)?

**Rule 15:**
- **Title:** Customer Reviews
- **Description:** Does the homepage contain general customer reviews?

**Rule 16:**
- **Title:** Main Products Visibility
- **Description:** Once you land on the homepage, do you immediately know the main products the store is selling?

**Rule 17:**
- **Title:** Store Ratings Display
- **Description:** Does the homepage show overall store ratings from authoritative review sites (Trustpilot, Yotpo, etc.)?
